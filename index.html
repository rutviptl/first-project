ABSTRACT ART

Abstract Art is a non-objective art, which does not have an accurate representation of a visual reality, but uses Forms, Colors, Textures and Gesture Marks to achieve its effect. It was born at the beginning of the 20th century and was completely radical for its time. Some may even say that abstraction started with cave paintings from thousands of years ago, Abstract Art represents an artistic break from traditional molds, that was the main feature.

Acrylic paint is a type of paint that is mixed with a water-soluble resin. They are typically used to create a layer of paint on a surface. Acrylic paint is typically made from acrylic resin, pigment, and dyes. This type of paint is typically used to create a layer of paint on a surface. The paints are used for a range of applications like posters, signs, signs and decorations, and home decoration.

GLASS PAINTING

See more ideas about this art

Glass paintings are executed with oil and hard resin or with watercolour and gum on glass sheets. These have been a folk art tradition in Europe and North America and, from the 15th to the 18th century, were regarded as a fine art. Glass painting or glass painter might refer to either technique, but more usually enamelled glass. It may also refer to the cinematic technique of matte painting, which is a type of painted representation of landscape.
